Welcome to Bayside! Come see this beautiful ALL red brick classic colonial. Home is nestled on a secluded lot outlined with mature tree borders. Extra deep lot allows room for elegant circular driveway and private backyard. Upper level of home features 3 HUGE bedrooms, all with walk-in closets, master suite and additional full bath. Main level features office/den, 1st floor laundry/mud room. Enjoy the custom kitchen, designed perfectly for cooking and entertaining guests. The dinette area has patio doors that open to stamped concrete patio with fire pit. Spacious great room with natural fireplace and a formal dining room.

Home features NEWER: roof, furnace, A/C,windows, soffit, fascia, gutters, drain tile, water heater, well pump & more.

Located 5 min from I-43 exit 10 min DT MKE. A must see!
